For this task, I used Canva to design a digital infographic on the topic of digital literacy. Canva was chosen because it provides easy-to-use templates, icons, and layout tools that make designing visually appealing content simple even for beginners.

My infographic presents key aspects of digital literacy, including its definition, core skills, safe internet practices, and building a professional online presence. It highlights important ideas such as protecting personal information, verifying online sources, and using platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub to showcase skills. The design uses a central theme with surrounding sections to clearly organize information and make it easy to understand at a glance.

One interesting aspect of creating this infographic was learning how to balance visual design with content clarity. Choosing colors, icons, and spacing that look good while still keeping the information readable was challenging. Overall, this task helped me better understand both digital literacy concepts and basic design principles.
